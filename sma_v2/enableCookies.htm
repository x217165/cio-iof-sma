In order to run SMA your browser needs to accept cookies.

If permited by your system administrator, you can order your browser to accept all cookies (please check with your system administrator if you are allowed to make these changes):

1. Choose Tools, then
2. Internet Options.
3. Click the Security tab, select "Local intranet",

4. Click Internet, then Custom Level.

5. Scroll down to Cookies and choose enable for both types
